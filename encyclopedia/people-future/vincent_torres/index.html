Hair Color: Blond

Eye Color: Blue

Physical Description:

General Description

Vincent is tall, athletic, and charismatic. He loves the small pleasures in life and does not play by other people’s rules. He has fallen deeply in love with Riana after only one meeting and looks forward to any time at all that they can spend together.

Skills of Note

Vincent is an accomplished brawler although he has never studied martial arts formally. He has learned whatever he can from anyone who would teach him. He is an exceptional pilot, reasonable computer hacker, and expert negotiator/facilitator.
