General Description

Calm, cool and collected. Vargrus was the pillar that held up the group when all else was falling down around them. Likewise he was also the infuriatingly calm and imperturbable one of the group. He was a fiercely loyal man who always held to his convictions no matter the pressures around him.

Vargrus was a human warrior who became the husband of Ambrai Valkyrie. Together they bore several children and eventually Vargrus died of natural causes.
